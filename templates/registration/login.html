GENERAL EXAM RULES
Please Read!!!

1. Students should be within the vicinity of the examination room 30 minutes before the examination is due.

2. Students are NOT PERMITTED to introduce into the examination room papers/books of any kind, hand bags, mobile phones or any programmable device.

3. Any student without University I.D card and a signed and stamped examination card SHALL NOT be allowed into the examination room.

4. Where a student is found writing the examination without the requirement in 3 above, that examination SHALL BE terminated.

5. For appropriate identification, student MUST NOT cover his/her face during the examination.

6. No student will be permitted to:

a. Enter the examination room if he/she is more than 30 minutes late

b. Leave the examination room before the end of 45 minutes after the commencement of the examination.

c. eave the examination room during the last 15 minutes of the examination

7. There should be NO writing on the exam card or any part of the student's body/cloth.

8. To attract the attention of the invigilator, students should raise their hands. Absolute silence MUST be maintained throughout the examination period.

9. Students are required to sign the attendance slip at their desk.

10. Movement in or around examination premises is strictly.

11. Wearing of p-cap and /or sun glasses is NOT allowed during examination room.

12. Any student with indecent dressing SHOULD NOT be allowed into examination room.

13. No student should be allowed to change seat without prior permission from invigilator.

15. Student are MANDATED to check around them and take away pieces of papers and any implicating material in their surroundings before the commencement of any examination.
